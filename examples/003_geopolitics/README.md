## Breakthrough Findings

### 1. GTA VI Delay Premium is Systematic
All three "before GTA VI" markets (Taiwan, Trump, Ceasefire) trade near 50-55% despite vastly different geopolitical events. The swarm quantified the delay premium:
- China invades: 51.5% market vs ~2% geopolitical leg = **~49% GTA VI premium**
- Trump out: 52.5% market vs ~4% political leg = **~48% GTA VI premium**
- Ceasefire: 53.5% market vs ~12% ceasefire leg = **~41% GTA VI premium**

### 2. Causal Chain Detection
Fish Quant and Bayesian both identified: **Putin out → ceasefire accelerated → Zelenskyy faces elections → Zelenskyy out**. These markets are positively correlated through this chain. A single event (Putin removal) cascades through three markets.

### 3. Narratives vs. Primary Sources (Journalist Fish)

- "Trump could be impeached" — House vote count gives the definitive answer

### 4. Ceasefire is the Most Uncertain Market
