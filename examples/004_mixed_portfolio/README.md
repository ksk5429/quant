## Breakthrough Findings

### 1. Netanyahu Is the Strongest Signal
All 8 Fish converged: Netanyahu's coalition is collapsing. UTJ withdrew (Journalist found this), elections are constitutionally mandatory by Oct 27, polls show 51-69 seat loss. Bayesian: prior 45% → posterior 60%. This is the highest-confidence actionable signal in the portfolio.

### 2. GPT-6 Naming Risk
The market asks "Will GPT-6 be released?" but OpenAI's "Spud" model may ship as GPT-5.5. The model capability exists; the branding decision doesn't. This is a semantic bet, not a technology bet — perfectly suited for our semantic prediction engine.

### 3. House Control Is Mispriced
Market says 85.5% Dem / 14.5% GOP. Swarm says 78.6% / 21.4%. The 7% gap comes from: midterm patterns fail 28% of the time, April polls miss November by 3+ points, GOP has a fundraising lead in key districts. Calibrator confirmed: both Fish estimates sum to exactly 100%.

### 4. 50-50 Clause Markets Are Solved
BTC $1M, Jesus, and (partially) GPT-6 are all dominated by the GTA VI delay → 50-50 resolution clause. The swarm correctly PASS on these — no informational edge possible when the resolution mechanism is mechanical.

### 5. Calibrator Caught a Bug
fish_contrarian estimated GPT-6 at 48% — BELOW the 50% mathematical floor imposed by the 50-50 clause. The Calibrator flagged this as "a logic bug, not useful contrarianism." This is exactly why Tier 3 exists.
